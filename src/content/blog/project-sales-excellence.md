I've held leadership positions with brands like Greenply, Greenlam, Nuwud, and Anchor. Across each role, one thread has remained constant: **project-based institutional sales** is where the largest, most durable business opportunities live.

## The Domain

My sector expertise spans the full spectrum of India's institutional construction:

- **Public Works & Defence** — CPWD, MES, Border Roads, Paramilitary Forces
- **Railways & Transport** — Indian Railways, State Transport Corporations, Metro projects
- **Education & Healthcare** — Kendriya Vidyalaya, AIIMS, state universities, district hospitals
- **Banking & Hospitality** — SBI, nationalised banks, ITDC, state tourism corporations
- **Housing & Development** — DDA, state development authorities, NBCC, group housing

Each of these segments operates through a different procurement mechanism — rate contracts, works contracts, direct specifications, or empanelment. Understanding which lever to pull, and when, is what separates systematic project sales from opportunistic order-taking.

## What It Takes

Winning in this space requires a unique combination of capabilities:

- **Forging business partnerships** with architects, consultants, and PMCs who influence specifications
- **Leading cross-functional teams** that bridge manufacturing, quality, logistics, and field sales
- **Exploring new markets** and expanding businesses from scratch in untested geographies
- **Streamlining operations** to ensure that the product delivered matches the product specified

In practice, this means working like a one-man army — managing bid processes, green certifications, quality inspections, and stakeholder alignment simultaneously.

## The Long Game

It took many years to understand the ins and outs of such a complicated role. Institutional sales in India is not a sprint. It's a relationship-driven, specification-driven, patience-driven discipline where the payoff compounds over decades.

The brands I've worked with have benefited not just from individual project wins, but from the ecosystem of approvals, specifications, and reference projects that make the next win easier than the last.
